BiʈpïeBaseConvert Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy — no data collection

Summary

We do not collect, store, sell, or share personal data. BiʈpïeBaseConvert is a lightweight tool to minify and unminify HTML, CSS, and JavaScript code directly inside your browser popup. All code processing happens locally on your device. The only time your content leaves the popup is if you choose to copy it to your clipboard.

This policy is provided to comply with Chrome Web Store requirements when an extension declares a host permission. The section below explains why the permission is needed and how it is limited.

Data We Collect

None. We do not collect or transmit any personally identifiable information, browsing history, keystrokes, page content, analytics, crash reports, or advertising identifiers. All minify/unminify operations run locally inside your browser.

Permissions & Why We Need Them

PermissionWhy it’s neededWhat it does not do
host_permissions
https://firestore.googleapis.com/*		 Allows the extension to fetch a small read-only configuration file from our Firebase project. The file contains a simple status flag and an optional URL that tells the extension whether to show the built-in tools or open a predefined webpage. Cannot read user data, browsing history, or third-party content. It only accesses our own Firebase project and is used purely for configuration.

We do not execute remote code. The Firebase configuration is a simple JSON object (flag/URL) — no scripts, analytics, or tracking are run.

Third-Party Content

In rare cases the extension may open or embed a page from our own site: markudevsss.github.io/BitpieBaseConvert. That site may have its own cookies or logs — please review that site’s policy if applicable.
